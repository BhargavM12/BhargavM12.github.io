P3: Part A: SEO Techniques

Title Tag: A title tag starts with the primary keyword, followed by a secondary keyword, and then the brand name. Meta title tags are crucial for search engines to grasp the content's topic, and they serve as the initial impression for most users.

Backlinks: Backlinks from reputable websites are among the most important factors for ranking. A webpage with numerous inbound links is more likely to rank higher on the SERP compared to a page without them.

Image Alt Text: alt text enhances accessibility for users who are unable to view images, such as those relying on screen readers or experiencing slow internet connections. It also helps algorithms, alongside computer vision, to interpret the image's content.

P3: Part A: SMO Techniques

Add Calls to Action: Using calls to action is a tried-and-true method for boosting audience interaction. Consider how digital creators often say something like, "What do you think is the best thing to put on toast? Share your thoughts in the comments" or "I love hearing from you!"

Master Hashtags: By incorporating hashtags into your content, you connect directly to the broader social ecosystem. They help categorize your posts and make them more discoverable, which is key for extending your reach beyond your known audience.

Post at the Right Times: Timing and frequency are key to social media success. The best time to post depends on your audience and platform. Adjust based on where your audience is.
